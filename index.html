About Me

Nice to meet you!

I’m Jan-Luca, Frontend Developer and UI Designer

Hi, my name is Jan-Luca (or just Luca) and I’m a Frontend Webdeveloper and UI Designer from Germany. I have over 3 years of Experience in developing digital products.

It’s very important to me to write code that’s easy to read as well as to maintain and to create awesome, individual designs.
My interest for webdevelopment already developed in my youth, so I started pursuing my own projects very early on.

My first project was a Pokemon fanpage where I would post news and other articles all around those games. I got my first experience with HTML and CSS. Over the years I expanded my knowledge and learned new things like WordPress and PHP. In School I got the chance to deepen everything I learned so far in the IT-Course.

After School I started my Career as a Webdeveloper at a digital agency. There I was mainly in Backend Development for two years. I also got my first experience with Frontend Development and UI Design and learned new things like JavaScript or how to properly use UI Tools like XD or Sketch.

In the last months of those first two years I realized, that I’m a lot more comfortable with Frontend rather than Backend, so I changed my Focus to Frontend Development entirely.
